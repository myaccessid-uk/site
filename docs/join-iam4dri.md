# Join the IAM4DRI Community

IAM4DRI is a UK community focused on Federated Identity and Access Management for Digital Research Infrastructures (DRIs). This list supports discussion, knowledge exchange, case studies, and problem-solving related federated access approaches. If you are interested, consider subscribing to the [IAM4DRI@JISCMAIL.AC.UK][mail-to-link] mailing list.
